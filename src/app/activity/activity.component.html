Welcome to CPS Notifications

CPhA's new product that allows Canadian Healthcare practitioners to access, browse and search for clinically relevant updates on drug products.

The repository below is the first of three deliverables. The second will be a preference centre that will allow you to receive emails, at a frequency you set, when notification is added. The third will present notifications in context in our drug content.
